How to Play

Minesweeper is a classic puzzle game where the objective is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden "mines" or bombs without detonating any of them. Use the numbers revealed in the cells to identify where the mines are located.

Game Rules

  • Left-click on a cell to reveal it.
  • Right-click on a cell to place a flag where you think a mine is located.
  • Numbers in revealed cells indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell.
  • Clear all non-mine cells to win the game.
  • If you reveal a mine, you lose the game.

Tips and Strategies

  • Use the numbers as clues to determine where the mines are located.
  • Place flags on suspected mines to avoid accidentally clicking on them.
  • Start by clicking around the edges to reveal larger areas of the board.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you play, the better you'll get.
